Algorithms
3 ML MODELS

Three different algorithms compete — the most accurate one wins and is used for predictions.

📈
Logistic Regression

Draws a mathematical boundary between approved and rejected applicants. Fast, interpretable, great baseline.

Accuracy~82%
🌳
Decision Tree

Asks a series of yes/no questions like a flowchart to reach a decision based on your financial data.

Accuracy~79%
🌲
Random Forest

Runs 100 decision trees and takes a majority vote. Most powerful — usually the winner.

Accuracy~85%
